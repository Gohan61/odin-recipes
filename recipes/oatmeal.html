Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal is a very healthy and delicious meal especially for breakfast. It is made out of grains that are sometimes flattened, hulled or de-husked. They can be cooked or eaten cold by mixing them with milk.

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Milk
  • Fruit

Steps

  1. Add the oatmeal to a pan
  2. Put on a low fire and mix it with the milk
  3. Slice up any fruit you prefer and slowly at it into the pan
  4. Cook for about two minutes
  5. Enjoy!
