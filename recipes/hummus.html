Hummus

Description

Hummus is a mediterranean recipes consisting of chickpeas. It can be eaten for breakfast, lunch or dinner It is quite healthy and tastes better when made fresh at home

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Tahini
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Water
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth, if not add olive oil or water
  3. Spread hummus over bread or eat with a spoon
