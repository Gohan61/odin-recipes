Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna with fillings between consisting of rag√π, bechamel sauce, vegetables, cheese and seasoning.

Lasagna originated in Italy during the Middle Ages. Over the years many altercations have gone on the original recipe.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna
  • Cheese
  • Minced meat
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Layer a sheet of lasagna
  2. Add the cooked meat, cheese and lasagna per layer
  3. Put the lasagna in the oven at 185 Celsius for one hour
  4. Enjoy!
