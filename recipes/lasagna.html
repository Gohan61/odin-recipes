Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta. It is made using stacked layers of lasagna with fillings of ragu, bechamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses and seasoning.

It is typically baked in an oven and cut in rectangular pieces

Ingredients

  • Lasagna layers
  • Ragu
  • Bechamel
  • Vegetables
  • Cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Lay first lasagna layer
  2. Spread Ingredients on first layer
  3. Keep going until tray full with every layer
  4. Set oven to 180 degrees and bake for an hour
  5. Cut lasagna in rectangular pieces
